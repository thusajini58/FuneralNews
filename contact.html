Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a memorial?

Click on the "Create Memorial" button in the header, fill out the form with the required details, and submit. Your memorial will be published after review.

Is there a cost to create a memorial?

We offer both free and premium memorial options. Basic memorials are free, while premium features include enhanced layouts and additional media options.

How long do memorials stay online?

All memorials remain online permanently at no additional cost. We believe in preserving memories forever.

Can I edit a memorial after it's published?

Yes, you can request edits by contacting us through this form or by calling our support line. We'll assist you with any changes needed.

How do I add tributes to a memorial?

Simply visit the memorial page and click "View / Add" button. You can write your tribute, share memories, and add photos.

Do you offer support in multiple languages?

Yes, we support English, Tamil, and Sinhala. You can switch languages using the language selector on the main page.
