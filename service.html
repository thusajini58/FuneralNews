What We Offer
01
Online Memorial Pages
Create beautiful digital memorial pages with photos, stories, tributes and life history. A permanent place online to remember and celebrate the life of your loved one.
02
Photo & Video Gallery
Upload cherished moments, albums and important life events in a clean gallery view. Preserve memories visually and share them with family and friends worldwide.
03
Tribute Wall
Family and friends can leave messages, candles, flowers and personalised tributes. A shared space for the community to come together and express their condolences.
04
Live Obituary Sharing
Share memorial pages instantly through social media, SMS and family groups. Reach loved ones across the world within minutes of publishing.
05
Funeral Service Details
Easily publish funeral dates, venues, livestreams, routes and guidance for attendees. Keep everyone informed and updated in one central place.
06
Remembrance Live Coverage
Professional live coverage of funeral proceedings available island-wide. Allow those who cannot attend in person to be present through a live stream.
07
Social Media & Media Publishing
Publish the obituary across Facebook, Instagram, newspapers and partner media websites. Ensure the announcement reaches the widest possible audience.
